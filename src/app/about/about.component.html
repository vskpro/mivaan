About us

Mivaan is a pharmaceutical company that concentrates on developing, manufacturing and producing a broad range of generic medicine at inexpensive prices. We put in our greatest effort to understand the needs of our customers. This empowers us to develop quality pharmaceutical/ cure-all products. Mivaan has over 100+ products being marketed and more than 100+ products under progress.

Our portfolio consists of a diverse range of formulations including tablets, injectables and creams for both humans and animals. Our product portfolio caters to a wide range of curative areas such as painkillers, antibiotics, allergy medicine, gastro-enterology management, blood pressure management and many more.

Mivaan has a deep-rooted solidity which lies in providing a distinguishable cost advantage to our consumers. As one of the fastest growing generic medicine companies in India, our mission is to fulfill our commitment to reaching out to as many people as possible and promoting a healthier life.
